Introduction

Our quiz is designed with accessibility in mind, ensuring an inclusive experience for all users. We provide high contrast mode for better readability, reduced motion support to minimize distractions, and keyboard navigation for users who rely on assistive technologies. Additionally, our quiz uses semantic HTML and ARIA attributes to improve screen reader compatibility, making it accessible to visually impaired users.
